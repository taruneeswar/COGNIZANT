# Learning Notes

## What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Examples:

- Image Recognition
- Speech Recognition
- Chatbots
- Recommendation Systems

---

## What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a type of Artificial Intelligence capable of generating new content such as:

- Text
- Images
- Audio
- Video
- Code

Examples include ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, and image generation models.

---

## Large Language Models (LLMs)

Large Language Models are AI models trained on massive amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like language.

Examples:

- GPT
- Llama
- Gemini
- Claude

---

## Prompt Engineering

Prompt Engineering is the practice of designing clear and effective prompts to obtain better responses from AI models.

Good prompts are:

- Clear
- Specific
- Context-rich
- Goal-oriented

---

## Tokens

A token is a unit of text processed by an AI model. Models read and generate responses token by token.

---

## Embeddings

Embeddings convert text into numerical vectors that capture semantic meaning, enabling similarity search and retrieval.

Applications:

- Semantic Search
- Recommendations
- Document Retrieval

---

## Vector Database

A Vector Database stores embeddings and enables efficient similarity searches.

Popular Vector Databases:

- Pinecone
- ChromaDB
- FAISS
- Milvus

---

## Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

RAG combines document retrieval with a Large Language Model to generate responses grounded in external knowledge.

Workflow:

1. User asks a question.
2. Query is converted into an embedding.
3. Relevant documents are retrieved from a vector database.
4. The retrieved context is provided to the LLM.
5. The LLM generates an informed response.

Benefits:

- More accurate answers
- Reduced hallucinations
- Up-to-date information

---

- Understand goals
