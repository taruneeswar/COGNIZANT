# Agile Terminologies

## Agile

An iterative software development methodology.

---

## Scrum

An Agile framework used to manage software projects.

---

## Sprint

A fixed-duration iteration in Scrum.

---

## Product Backlog

A prioritized list of product requirements maintained by the Product Owner.

---

## Sprint Backlog

Tasks selected for completion during a sprint.

---

## Increment

The working software delivered at the end of a sprint.

---

## Product Owner

Responsible for maximizing product value and managing the Product Backlog.

---

## Scrum Master

Facilitates Scrum practices and removes obstacles.

---

## Development Team

A cross-functional team responsible for delivering the product.

---

## Daily Scrum

A 15-minute daily meeting to discuss progress and plan work.

---

## Sprint Planning

Meeting where the team plans work for the upcoming sprint.

---

## Sprint Review

Meeting to demonstrate completed work to stakeholders.

---

## Sprint Retrospective

Meeting to reflect on the sprint and identify improvements.

---

## User Story

A simple description of a feature from the user's perspective.

---

## Epic

A large feature that can be divided into multiple User Stories.

---

## Story Points

A relative measure of effort required to complete a User Story.

---

## Velocity

The amount of work completed by a Scrum Team in one sprint.

---

## Burndown Chart

A chart showing the remaining work during a sprint.
